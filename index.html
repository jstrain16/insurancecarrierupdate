The Breakdown

Four carriers, four moves to know

Commercial · BOP / Property / Auto
Travelers
  • Eff Apr 12 Contractors (concrete, tile, masonry, paving) moved to a .75 dev factor — a 25% pricing savings, on top of the additional 25% scheduled credit you can still apply.
  • Eff Mar 15 Manufacturing & Wholesale dropped to the same .75 for a 25% savings — a 3-month trial. Lead with metal mfg, cabinet/countertop shops, and tech. (Full UW review.)
  • Eff Jan 11 Opened low-exposure contractor classes for BOP 2.0 — painting, power washing, sheet metal — in AZ, CO, OR, WA, and MT.
  • Eff Mar 27 TCAP auto moved to .85 for contractors to sharpen multiline pricing; monoline auto needs 5+ light-to-medium power units.
Do this: Send Josh your contractor and manufacturing accounts and let him know what the premiums come in at.
Workers' Compensation
Selective
  • WC commission incentive: 20% on the first $25,000 in premium for preferred WC classes, then 10% at renewal.
  • Preferred classes: retail shops, medical & dental offices, banks/credit institutions, wholesale, schools, and LRO.
  • Bonus: bind $10,000 in premium through the 1-and-done / 2-and-done small-business portal and the rep covers a company-store item — notify Matthew Fish or Jessica McCue once you hit it.
Do this: Lean into preferred WC and send Josh your comparisons.
Commercial Auto · Utah
Berkshire Hathaway Homestate (BHHC)
  • Top-performing UT classes right now: Non-Emergency Medical Transportation (for-profit, non-profit, and new ventures — prefers permanently attached medical equipment / lifts).
  • Tow Trucks & Tilt-Bed Auto Haulers — confirm body type before submitting.
  • Day Care Center Autos — flagged as a growth class for Utah.
  • Rate 1–10 power units in the Breeze Auto Rater; 11+ fleet submissions go to auto@bhhomestate.com.
Do this: Pull eligible auto risks and quote them in Breeze.
E&S · Special Events
Cincinnati Specialty Underwriters (CSU)
  • Will now write special event policies with liquor liability — coverage is not available on BYOB events.
  • Most placements can go through their quick-issue team; a special-event application is on file with the carrier desk.
Do this: Bring Josh your special-event opportunities that need liquor liability.
